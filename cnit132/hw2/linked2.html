Animals of San Francisco

Many animals live in the wilds of San Francisco. Racoons, pigeons, mice, skunks, seagulls, and parrots, but my personal favorite is the snowy plover.

What is a Snowy Plover?

A drink from Andytown. It's a shot of espresso poured over ice, then filled with sparkling water and topped with fresh whip cream. It sounds gross, but people wait in long lines and pay $5 for them so you know it's good.

Other variations:

It's also a little bird that likes to run around the beach. I'm not sure how much it costs, but I'm sure it would taste delicious with espresso, sparkling water, and whipped cream.
